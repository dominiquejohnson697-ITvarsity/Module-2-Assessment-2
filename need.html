FILM SYNOPSIS

Tobey Marshall (Aaron Paul), a mechanic, races muscle car in an underground circuit. Struggling to keep his business afloat, he reluctantly partners with wealthy but treacherous Dino Brewster (Dominic Cooper). However, Dino frames Tobey for a crime that sends him to jail. Tobey is out for revenge as soon as he's released from prison.
