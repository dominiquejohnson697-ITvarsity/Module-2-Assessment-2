WELCOME TO THE PINE CITY MALL ABOUT PAGE.


Pine City Mall offers more than 1 600 parking bays that are clean, safe and secure, so you'll never need to stress about finding parking again. Designated bays are available for the disabled, as well as for moms with toddlers.


Safety & Security

PINE City Mall has a 24-hour CCTV surveillance system covering both the internal and external areas, which is monitored via a state of the art security control facility and linked to emergency services. Parking areas are protected by foot and bicycle patrols and there are designated security guards throughout the mall. The centre has a fully equipped first aid room, with wheelchairs available for the disabled.
